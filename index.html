Brain Impact

Drugs disrupt brain function, impairing memory, decision-making, and emotional regulation. Chronic use can cause irreversible damage to neural pathways that are essential for healthy cognitive function.

Physical Health

Drug abuse damages vital organs, weakens immunity, and increases chronic disease risk. From heart and lung damage to deteriorating liver function, substance abuse takes a severe toll on physical wellbeing.
